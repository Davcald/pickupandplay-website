About Pickup and Play

Pickup and Play is a free community-driven platform that helps you discover and join local pickup sports games. Whether you're looking for a casual match or just want to stay active, our app makes it easy to find nearby games without any cost or hassle.

Features

Create Events

Easily schedule games and events with our intuitive interface.

Join Games

Find local games that match your interests and join with a single click.

Real-Time Updates

Receive instant notifications about any event changes or new games nearby.
